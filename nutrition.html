Nutrition

Nutrition is the cornerstone of fitness. It fuels your workouts and aids in recovery.
80% of your results comes from what you eat, while 20% comes from your workouts.
The 4 main food groups are: Proteins, Carbohydrates, Fats and Vitamins & Minerals.
Each group plays a crucial role in your overall health and fitness

Proteins

Provide the building blocks for building muscle. Essential for muscle repair and growth. Choose sources like chicken, beef, fish, beans and nuts etc

Carbohydrates

The body's main energy source. These fuel your activity and workouts. Choose sources like whole grains ie rice, pasta, quinoa, fruits ie apples, oranges, pears and vegetables ie broccoli, spinach, carrots etc

Fats

Important for normal hormone production and nutrient absorption. Choose healthy fats like avocados, olive oil, avocado oil etc

Vitamins & Minerals

Vital for various bodily functions. Ensure a colorful diet rich in fruits and vegetables. Choose sources like spinach, kale, carrots, berries, oranges, bananas etc
